# Gmail App Password Setup Guide

1. Enable 2-Step Verification on your Google Account.
2. Go to Google Account → Security → App Passwords.
3. Select 'Mail' as the app and 'Other (Custom name)' as the device.
4. Enter a name like "AutoJobMailer" and generate.
5. Copy the 16-character password and use it in the AutoJobMailer app
   instead of your normal Gmail password.
6. Keep this password safe. Do NOT share publicly.
